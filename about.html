Our Story

Founded in 2018, Uzima Cleaners Kenya began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and environmental responsibility.

What started as a small team of three cleaners has grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted cleaning companies, serving hundreds of satisfied residential and commercial clients across the city.

The name "Uzima" means "life" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to creating clean, healthy living and working environments that enhance quality of life for our clients.
