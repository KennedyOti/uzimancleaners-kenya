Specialized Services

Beyond our standard services, we offer specialized cleaning solutions to meet your unique needs. These services can be scheduled individually or added to your regular cleaning plan.

  • Professional carpet cleaning
  • Upholstery and furniture cleaning
  • Window washing (interior and exterior)
  • Post-construction cleanup
  • Pressure washing (exterior surfaces)

Our professional carpet cleaning service uses hot water extraction to remove deep-seated dirt, stains, and allergens from your carpets. We treat all types of carpets and area rugs, extending their life and improving indoor air quality.

  • Hot water extraction (steam cleaning)
  • Pre-treatment of stains
  • Deodorizing treatment available
  • Fast drying technology

Refresh your furniture with our professional upholstery cleaning service. We carefully clean all types of fabric furniture, removing dirt, stains, and odors while protecting the integrity of your upholstery.

  • Fabric-safe cleaning solutions
  • Spot and stain treatment
  • Leather cleaning available
  • Deodorizing options

Our window cleaning service leaves your windows streak-free and sparkling, both inside and out. We clean window frames, tracks, and glass for a complete refresh of your view.

  • Interior and exterior window washing
  • Frame and track cleaning
  • Screen cleaning available
  • Hard water stain removal
  • High-rise window cleaning with safety equipment

After construction or renovation, your space needs thorough cleaning to remove dust and debris. Our post-construction cleanup service ensures your space is move-in ready.

  • Fine dust removal from all surfaces
  • Debris cleanup
  • Sticky residue removal
  • Final touch cleaning
  • Waste disposal coordination
